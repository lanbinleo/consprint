Set A: Research Variables & Logic
Independent variables
oThe factor manipulated by the researcher to determine its effect. 
o研究咖啡因对记忆的影响，咖啡因摄入量就是自变量
Dependent variables
oThe outcome factor that is measured; the variable that may change in response to manipulations. 
o在上述研究中，记忆力测试的分数就是因变量
Confounding variables
oFactors other than the independent variable that might produce an effect in an experiment. 
o参与者的睡眠质量可能会干扰对咖啡因效果的判断
Random assignment
oAssigning participants to experimental and control groups by chance. 
o通过抽签决定谁喝真咖啡，谁喝无咖啡因饮料
o判断是否是experiment的key point
Hypothesis
oA testable prediction, often implied by a theory. 
o假设：摄入咖啡因的学生在记忆测试中表现更好
Generalizability
oThe extent to which research findings can be applied to the broader population. 
o研究结果是否能从这10名学生推及到全校学生
Quantitative data
oInformation that can be measured and written down with numbers. 
o记录测试得到的具体分数
Qualitative data
oDescriptive data used to understand concepts, thoughts, or experiences.
o记录参与者对测试过程的主观感受描述
Set B: Ethical Guidelines
Institutional review
oA committee that reviews research proposals for ethical compliance. 
o实验开始前必须提交给IRB审核
Informed consent
oGiving potential participants enough information about a study to enable them to choose whether they wish to participate.
o实验前让学生了解流程并签署同意书
Informed assent
oAgreement to participate from a minor or someone unable to give legal consent
o针对未成年人，除了家长同意，还需要孩子本人表达参与意愿
oDavid，不要再拼错了
Protection from harm
oEnsuring participants do not endure any unnecessary physical or psychological distress.
o确保实验不会对学生造成持久的心理压力
Confidentiality
oKeeping all information about participants private and anonymous.
o发布数据时隐去学生姓名，只用编号代替，只看数据没办法知道是谁的
Deception
oIntentionally misleading participants about the true nature of the experiment. 
o告诉学生是在测视力，实际在测从众行为
oProper deception is acceptable，但无意义的deception会被IRB驳回
Debriefing
oThe post-experimental explanation of a study, including its purpose and any deceptions.
o实验结束后，老师真相大白，解释实验目的和流程，if有deception，需要解释原因
o无论有没有deception，都需要有debriefing的环节

Set C & D: Statistics
Central tendency
oA single score that represents a whole set of scores. 
o使用一个数值来概括全班的整体表现
o包括3M
Mean
oThe arithmetic average of a distribution. 
o把全班分数加起来除以总人数
Median
oThe middle score in a distribution. 
o将全班分数从低到高排列，找到最中间那个数
Mode
oThe most frequently occurring score(s) in a distribution. 
o班里出现次数最多的分数，比如有10个人都考了85分
o如果都只出现了一次，则本data set没有mode
Variation
oHow similar or diverse the scores are. 
o观察班级内学生分数的差异程度
Percentile rank
oThe percentage of scores in a distribution that are lower than or equal to a specific score. 
o你的分数超过了全校 90% 的人
Range
oThe difference between the highest and lowest scores in a distribution. 
o最高分98减去最低分40，range是58
Normal curve
oA symmetrical, bell-shaped curve that describes the distribution of many types of data. 
o大多数人的身高都集中在平均值附近
o只要sample size够大，很多characteristics都会是normal distribution
Bimodal distribution
oA distribution with two clear peaks. 
o一天车流量明显有两个高峰，早高峰和晚高峰
Standard deviation
oA computed measure of how much scores vary around the mean score. 
o反映分数是紧贴平均分还是离散得很远
Regression toward the mean
oThe tendency for extreme scores to fall back toward their average. 
o第一次考了破纪录高分的球星，下次表现往往会回归平庸
Positive skew
oA distribution where the tail points toward the higher values (right). 
o题目太难导致大部分人不及格，只有极少数人考了高分
oMean > Median
Negative skew
oA distribution where the tail points toward the lower values (left). 
o题目太简单导致大部分人都拿高分，只有极少数人不及格
oMean < Median
Scatterplot
oA graphed cluster of dots representing the values of two variables. 
o在图表上画出身高与鞋码对应的数据点
oCorrelational study 画出data的方式
Correlation
oA measure of the extent to which two factors vary together. 
o研究观看暴力电视与攻击性行为之间的联系
oMoses 上了AP Psych就被斯坦福录了，所以能得出什么结论呢？
oCorrelation中不分IV / DV
Positive correlation
oBoth variables increase or decrease together. 
o复习时间越多，考试成绩越高
Negative correlation
oAs one variable increases, the other decreases. 
o缺勤次数越多，考试成绩越低
o两个variables的变化方向不一致
Correlation coefficient
oA statistical index of the relationship between two things (from -1.00 to +1.00). 
or = +0.85，说明两者有很强的正相关
oAAQ Question C 分析r时，需要说明direction + strength

Set E: Sampling & Experimental Design
Population
oAll those in a group being studied, from which samples may be drawn. 
o研究的对象是全中国所有的高中生
o所有的potential samples
Sample
oA subset of the population used for the actual study. 
o实际参与实验的500名学生
Representative sample
oA sample that accurately reflects the characteristics of the population. 
o样本中男女比例、地区分布要与全国情况一致
o有代表性的sample才能generalize to larger population
oDemographic factors 要多样
Random sampling
oA sample that fairly represents a population because each member has an equal chance of inclusion. 
o通过电脑随机生成的名单来抽取受试者
o和random assignment的区别不想再说，别看错了球球了
Convenience sampling
oChoosing individuals who are easiest to reach. 
o为了省事，只调查了Psych班里的学生
Representation of participants
oThe degree to which the sample matches the population. 
o这500名受试者是否能代表全体高中生？需要判断一下degree of generalizability
Experimental group
oThe group exposed to the treatment (independent variable). 
o在实验中服用新开发药物的那组人
Control group 
oThe group not exposed to the treatment, serving as a comparison. 
o在实验中服用安慰剂的那组人
o吃placebo的
Single-blind study
oParticipants do not know whether they are in the experimental or control group. 
o病人不知道自己吃的是真药还是小糖片
Double-blind study
oNeither the participants nor the researchers know who is in which group. 
o学生和代课老师都不知道哪组是实验组
oAvoid experimenter bias （你现在有什么感觉吗？真的没有吗~~ 被试：？）

Set F: Research Methods & Methodology
Operational definition
oA carefully worded statement of the exact procedures used in a research study. 
o将学习压力定义为唾液中cortisol的含量
o让读者or后续的研究人员知道你到底是怎么测量variables的
o记得找questionnaire或者是任何的quantifiable measurement！不过在AAQ Question B, 是让你找文章中的OD，不是你凭空想一个
Case study
oA descriptive technique in which one individual or group is studied in depth. 
o对一名大脑受损的罕见病例进行长达十年的追踪研究
oRare / unethical to duplicate 
oH.M. for anterograde amnesia, Broca’s / Wernicke’s aphasia, prefrontal cortex study
Meta-analysis
oA statistical procedure for combining and analyzing data from many different studies.
o综合过去20年里所有关于睡眠与记忆的研究得出结论
Naturalistic observation
oObserving and recording behavior in naturally occurring situations without manipulation. 
o在操场上偷偷观察并记录小朋友玩耍时的社交互动
o睁大你的眼睛看看怎么拼的哈，不要再给我写什么natural study了
Structured interviews
oA research procedure in which all participants are asked the same set of questions.
o对所有应聘者统一询问三个固定问题
o别自由发挥，不让confounding variables影响结果
Likert scale
oA survey scale used to measure attitudes or opinions (e.g., 1-5 scale). 
o请对Irene的AP Psych满意度打分，1分是非常不满，5分是非常满意
Effect size
oThe strength of the relationship between two variables. 
o虽然结果显著，但这种药对减肥的实际效果大小只有0.5斤
oPearson’s r是一种effect size
Statistical significance
oA statistical statement of how likely it is that an obtained result occurred by chance. 
op值小于0.05（不需要记，只是写到这方便理解）说明实验结果不是乱撞上的，不是by chance而是真实的情况
Replication
oRepeating the essence of a research study with different participants. 
o在中国做完实验后，去美国用同样的步骤再做一次
oReplication的前提是有非常清楚的OD
Peer review
oEvaluation of scientific work by others working in the same field. 
o论文发表前由其他心理学专家进行盲审和挑错

Set G: Cognitive Biases & Research Problems
Falsifiability
oThe possibility that a hypothesis can be proven false. 
o科学假设必须是可以被证伪的，比如：我们坚信小栗小蛋糕是最可爱的，这个导致了你考5分，这个就没办法证伪or证实，所以这个不是科学
oPsychoanalysis / psychodynamics的许多理论就因为缺乏falsifiability而受到批评
Placebo
oAn inert substance or condition given to the control group. 
o给control group吃外表一模一样的淀粉片
Placebo effect
oExperimental results caused by expectations alone. 
o病人以为吃了药，结果心理作用导致病情真的好转了
oSingle blind就可以做到避免这个，不过实际能做到double blind更好
Overconfidence
oThe tendency to be more confident than correct. 
o学生觉得自己复习得很好能考5分，结果实际只考了3分
Directionality problem
oWhen a correlation exists, it’s unclear which variable caused the change in the other. 
o是焦虑导致了失眠，还是失眠导致了焦虑？
Third variable problem
oAn external variable that leads to a mistaken correlation between two factors. 
o夏天冰淇淋销量和溺水人数正相关，其实是因为气温升高这个variable
Experimenter bias
oWhen a researcher’s expectations influence the results. 
o老师潜意识里希望实验成功，从而不自觉地鼓励了实验组
Social desirability bias
oGiving answers that make one look good in the eyes of others. 
o在调查问卷中谎称自己从不撒谎
Cultural norms
oThe rules and expectations of a society. 
o中国人过年给红包是一种norm，来都来了大过年的给孩子的
oDisplay rules是cultural norms在emotion expression的体现
Confirmation bias
oA tendency to search for information that supports our preconceptions. 
o只关注能证明玩游戏有害的新闻，无视能证明有益的研究
Hindsight bias
oThe tendency to believe, after learning an outcome, that one would have foreseen it. 
o考试答案公布是C，你拍大腿说：我当时就说选C吧 :)
